01 — the trip

Why shoulder
season.

12 days · 14 stops · 412 mi

We had been waiting for weather that nobody else wanted. The lodges open the first week of April, the roads through Tower Junction clear by the second, and for those nine or ten days the park is yours alone — half-frozen, half-thawed, and almost entirely without other rigs in the campgrounds.

This loop is built around the Lamar Valley at first light, three nights at Madison Junction in the middle, and a slow drift south to the Tetons in the last week. Cell service is intermittent. Most of these stops were uploaded a day or two later from a coffee shop in West Yellowstone.
