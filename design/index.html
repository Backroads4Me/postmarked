02 — direction

A field journal,
not a SaaS dashboard.

The reading experience borrows from print: an editorial serif at display sizes, a humanist sans for body, and a monospace reserved for the things that should look like data — coordinates, dates, distances, file metadata. Photos run full bleed. Cards do not box themselves; hairlines and whitespace do the work.

A single warm accent — campfire amber — marks the live signal: where the rig is now, the active route, the publisher’s voice. Everything else is alpine ink and warm paper.

Principles
  • 01 Photos and the map are the heroes. Chrome recedes.
  • 02 One accent. Use it for the live signal, not decoration.
  • 03 Mono for data, serif for voice, sans for interface.
  • 04 Hairlines, not boxes. Whitespace, not borders.
  • 05 Mobile is the canonical reader. Bottom tab bar, no hamburger.
